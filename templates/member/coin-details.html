TRX

Tron (TRX) is a block-chain based dispersed platform having a cryptocurrency of its own. Tron came into existence in Asia but now had gone international. There are more than 50 million accounts on TRX as of the current reports.

The platform is developed by Justin Sun, he is currently the CEO of Tron. Tron now has offices in Singapore as well as San Fransisco. Among all the cryptocurrencies TRX has the 31st position.

The slogan of TRX is "Decentralize the web" and the team targets to discharge corporate agents from the most common way of consuming media.
